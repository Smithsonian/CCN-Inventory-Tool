We estimate tidal marsh area in Belize to be 4147 to 7740 hectares, with a mean estimate of hectares according to Worthington et al. (2024).

We estimate seagrass area to be Belize to be a mean of 4.848^{5} hectares, according to McKenzie et al. (2020), which aggregates global seagrass data from a number of sources.
