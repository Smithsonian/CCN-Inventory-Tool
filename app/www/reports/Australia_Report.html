Calculated Stocks and Emissions Factors

This section of the report details whether data is available to estimate Tier I, Tier II, or Tier III value estimates for tidal marsh, mangrove, and seagrass ecosystems in Australia.

If data for the selected country is available in the Coastal Carbon Atlas, we have applied a Tier II emission factor based on a simple average of country specific data queried from the Atlas.

Data from Australia included in the Coastal Carbon Atlas consists of 181 soil profiles from 135 watersheds. This data comes from 4 distinct habitat type(s).
